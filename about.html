My Goals with Design...

Understand the User

Throughout my experiences designing for different companies, startups, and projects, I've discovered that as long as you want to make an impact with the technology you are designing, you have to understand users' needs and painpoints in order to create the best experiences. This can be accomplished through usability testing, user interviews, and surveys

Accessibility

Even if you create the most innovative software or new technology, it won't be utilized to its fullest potential if users aren't able to access it. Accessibility is a core aspect of my design philosophy. I strive to create inclusive user experiences that consider diverse needs and ensure that everyone, regardless of abilities or disabilities, can interact seamlessly with the digital solutions I design.
